# Privacy Policy - LinkedIn Easy Apply Assistant

Effective date: 2026-02-06

## Overview

LinkedIn Easy Apply Assistant stores the profile information you enter in the extension popup to help fill application fields on LinkedIn Easy Apply pages.

## Data handling

- Storage location: `chrome.storage.local` (local browser profile on your device).
- Purpose: Fill user-initiated job application fields.
- Remote transfer by developer: None.
- Sale of data: None.
- Sharing with third parties by developer: None.

## Sensitive fields

The extension may store fields you choose to enter, including contact details and optional equal-employment-related fields (for example gender, race/ethnicity, veteran/disability status). These fields are only used for form filling behavior in the extension.

## User control

- You control what data is entered and saved.
- You can clear saved data by removing or editing values in the extension popup and saving again, or removing the extension.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the publisher through the Chrome Web Store support channel for this extension listing.
